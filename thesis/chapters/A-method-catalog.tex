\chapter{The \texorpdfstring{\maxm{*}}{max/*} Method Catalogue}
\label{app:methods}

\Cref{tab:maxm-full} catalogues the custom \maxm{*} JSON-RPC surface of the
artifact (\cref{ch:artifact}), as declared in
\code{lsp-max-protocol/src/custom\_methods.rs} and neighbouring modules
\citep{lspmax}. The surface is the operational form of the law-state runtime: it
exposes snapshots, three-valued admission and refusal, receipts, repair plans, and
release actuation over the same transport an editor would use for ordinary
language intelligence.

\noindent Three properties of the catalogue recur in the thesis. Admission
(\maxm{admission}) and refusal (\maxm{refusal}) are symmetric and both
evidence-bound; release (\maxm{releaseActuation}) is gated on the
\code{ConformanceVector}; and replay (\maxm{replay}) reconstructs state from the
receipt ledger, realising \cref{prop:replay-lfp}. The full surface is read-only
toward client files: any mutation routes through the \code{CodeAction}
$\to$ clap-noun-verb $\to$ receipt chain of \cref{sec:art-layers}.
